📌 Job Search Tips
📄
Keep Your Resume Updated
Always have an updated resume ready with your latest skills, experience, and contact details before applying.
🎯
Read the Job Description Carefully
Check eligibility, qualifications, age limit, and required documents before applying for any position.
⏰
Apply Before the Last Date
Never wait till the last moment. Server traffic increases near deadlines and you may miss your chance.
📸
Keep Documents Ready
Keep scanned copies of Aadhaar, certificates, photo, and signature ready in the required format and size.
🔔
Save the Confirmation
After applying, always download and save your application confirmation and payment receipt for future reference.
